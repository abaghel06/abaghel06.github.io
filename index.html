I started out building Appian-based compliance systems for Citibank at Wipro — work that meant understanding messy real-world processes and making them actually run reliably. One integration I pioneered cut remediation cycle time by 66% and became the go-to approach for the whole client team.

A couple of years in, I got curious about what happens when you bring AI reasoning into those kinds of systems. Not AI as a chatbot sitting on top of a workflow — but AI as part of the logic itself. That curiosity led me through IBM's RAG Specialization, AWS's GenAI Developer program, and eventually to building things myself.

I'm looking for a team where I can combine enterprise systems thinking with serious AI engineering. If that sounds like something you're building, I'd love to talk.
