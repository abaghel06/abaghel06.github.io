Personal Project

LoanIQ — Intelligent Loan Approval Assistant

Loan decisions aren't just about a credit score — they involve income stability, debt ratios, regulatory compliance, and edge cases that break simple rules. LoanIQ automates that entire evaluation with a pipeline of four specialised AI agents, each responsible for one layer.

An Applicant Profile Agent, Financial Risk Agent, Loan Decision Agent, and Compliance Agent work in sequence — each backed by its own MCP server. The output is a structured decision (Approve / Reject / Manual Review) with a risk score, confidence level, and a compliance case ID.
